Welcome to SA Bitcoin Tax

This tool helps South African Bitcoin users calculate their disposal events for tax reporting purposes. Connect your Blink wallet or upload a CSV of transactions to get started.

Privacy: All data — API keys, transaction history, and price data — is stored exclusively in your browser using IndexedDB. Nothing is ever transmitted to any server other than directly to the services you connect (Blink, Binance, CoinGecko, Frankfurter/ECB).

Disclaimer: This tool is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, tax, or legal advice. You are solely responsible for verifying all calculations and ensuring compliance with SARS requirements. Consult a qualified tax practitioner for advice specific to your situation.

Accuracy: Tax calculations depend on price data from third-party sources. Prices may be incomplete or inaccurate. Always verify figures against official sources before submitting a tax return.

Data loss: Clearing your browser data will permanently delete all stored accounts, transactions, and price history. Export or back up data before clearing browser storage.
